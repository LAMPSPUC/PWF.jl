DSHL

Description

AC circuit shunt data. This section enables association between shunt devices and AC circuits, facilitating failure simultation. This devices are automatically removed or inserted alongside the associated circuit.

Usage

ColumnDescription
From busBus number from one circuit extremity, as defined in DBAR
OperationShunt addition, removal or modification
To busBus number from the other circuit extremity, as defined in DBAR
CircuitIdentification number of the AC circuit
Shunt fromShunt reactive power in 'from bus' extremity for nominal voltage (1.0 p.u.) (Mvar)
Shunt toShunt reactive power in 'to bus' extremity for nominal voltage (1.0 p.u.) (Mvar)
Status fromOn or off status for 'from bus' extremity
Status toOn or off status for 'to bus' extremity
